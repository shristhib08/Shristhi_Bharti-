Udaipur in Summer


A summer tour to Udaipur (April-June) offers a quieter, budget-friendly experience focusing on lakes and evening
activities to beat the heat, with temperatures often exceeding. Key summer activities include boating on Lake Pichola,
visiting the air-conditioned City Palace, exploring the shaded Saheliyon-ki-Bari, and enjoying sunset views from the Monsoon Palace.




Best Summer Highlights & Activities

Lakeside Evenings: Take a sunset boat ride on Lake Pichola or Fateh Sagar Lake to enjoy cooler breezes.
Cultural Evenings: Visit Bagore Ki Haveli for evening dance performances.
Cool Shaded Spots: Explore Saheliyon Ki Bari, a garden with fountains and trees designed for relaxation.
Indoor Exploration: Tour the City Palace Museum or the Vintage Car Museum during the hottest part of the day.
Sajjangarh Monsoon Palace (Sajjangarh): A hilltop palace designed to watch clouds, offering panoramic views of the city and lakes in the mist.


Monsoon Travel Tips

Stay Hydrated: Drink plenty of water; carry sunscreen and sunglasses.
Timing: Sightsee in the early morning or after 4 PM, spending midday indoors or lounging in air-conditioned cafes.
Accommodation: Choose hotels with swimming pools to relax.
