Udaipur in Monsoon


Udaipur in the monsoon (July–September) is a magical experience, as the city's lakes fill to capacity and the
surrounding Aravalli hills turn lush green. Key highlights include the stunning, cloud-covered Sajjangarh Monsoon Palace,
romantic boat rides on Lake Pichola, visiting the revitalized Saheliyon ki Bari, and exploring the verdant, mist-covered outskirts like Alsigarh and Rayta Hills.




Top attractions in Udaipur


Sajjangarh Monsoon Palace (Sajjangarh): A hilltop palace designed to watch clouds, offering panoramic views of the city and lakes in the mist.
Lake Pichola & Jag Mandir: Take a boat ride to see the lakes at their fullest and witness the reflection of palaces on the water.
Bahubali Hills & Badi Lake: Offers scenic views and a popular, short trek with beautiful green landscapes, perfect for photography.
Alsigarh & Rayta Hills: Picturesque, lush green areas on the outskirts ideal for a scenic long drive.




Why Visit Udaipur in Monsoon?

Lush Landscapes: The arid Rajasthan landscape transforms into a vibrant green paradise.Atmospheric
Experience: The city is quieter and more romantic, with misty clouds often surrounding the palaces.
Less Crowded: You can enjoy the sights, such as the City Palace and lakes, without the peak season crowds.
Comfortable Climate: The rain brings a much-needed break from the summer heat, making sightseeing comfortable.

Monsoon Travel Tips

Best Time: Late July to early September is ideal as the heavy rainfall fills the lakes, often mentioned as the peak.
Stay: Book resorts or heritage hotels with lake or hill views, such as those recommended by StayVista, to maximize the, scenic experience.
Pack Accordingly: Bring umbrellas, raincoats, and appropriate footwear for walking in slippery conditions.
Enjoy Local Food: Savor hot Pyaaz Kachori and Chai while looking out at the rain-drenched city.

Estimated Costs & Logistics

Sajjangarh Palace Entry: ~₹136 INR per person, with around ~₹380 for car entry.
City Palace Entry: ~₹100 per person (audio tour available for extra cost).
