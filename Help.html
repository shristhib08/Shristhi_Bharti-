Worth a visit

Ambrai Ghat is one of the most peaceful and scenic spots in Udaipur. The view of Lake Pichola
with the City Palace in the background is absolutely beautiful, especially during sunset.
The reflection of the palace lights on the water in the evening creates a magical atmosphere.
It’s a perfect place to sit quietly, relax, and enjoy the calm surroundings.
